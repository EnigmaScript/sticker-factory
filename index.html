About The Sticker Factory

The Sticker Factory was started by a 10-year-old sticker enthusiast who wanted to share awesome stickers with friends. All our stickers are designed with fun and creativity in mind!

We use eco-friendly materials and our stickers are waterproof, scratch-resistant, and made to last. Perfect for decorating notebooks, laptops, water bottles, and more!
